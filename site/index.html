    What can this evidence prove today?

    Short answer: it can expose one-note problems and show historical failure rates from the launch baselines. It cannot crown today's best AI scribe yet. Use the cards below to decide what a result can support before you make a claim.

    Four launch rows scored all 57 PriMock57 consults with declared repeats and judge. They prove the harness works and show the failure gradient.

    Can show

    The Lab and smoke rows prove the public path can catch unsupported care and run current models. They are useful QA receipts, not rankings.

    Cannot claim yet

    Best current AI scribe

    Current model, vendor, and real-workflow claims need new powered rows with aggregate scores, judge, repeats, date, and tuning disclosure.

    No current-model crown yet: old GPT-4o and launch Claude rows stay visible as historical baselines because deleting them would hide evidence. They should be beaten, replaced, and contextualized by new powered runs.

            Method
            
          
            Three checks, one public scoring harness
            
          
            
            ScribeBench separates quality from safety. A note can be fluent and complete while
            still inventing something clinically meaningful, so the fabrication review is run as
            a separate adversarial judge.
          
            
        
            
        
            
          
            
            01
            
            Narrative quality
            
            
            Six physician-style dimensions: story, completeness, flow, artifacts, readability, and input fidelity.
            
          
            
          
            
            02
            
            Fabrication review
            
            
            Flags unsupported clinical content and separates dangerous invention from standard charting.
            
          
            
          
            
            03
            
            Leak detection
            
            
            Deterministic scan for raw template placeholders and internal metadata tokens.
